Privacy Policy (English Version)

At AbboBuddy, we highly value your privacy. Below you can read how we handle your data.

No Sharing of Personal Data

AbboBuddy does not share any personal data with third parties. Your data remains completely private and is never forwarded or sold to other parties.

Local Data Storage

All data you enter into AbboBuddy is stored exclusively locally on your own device. No data is sent to external servers.

Basic Functionality

AbboBuddy only offers basic features, such as managing subscriptions and sending notifications when this feature is enabled by the user. No additional data is collected or processed.

Data Deletion

You always retain control over your data. Want to delete all your data? Simply uninstall the app from your device. This will permanently delete all locally stored data.
